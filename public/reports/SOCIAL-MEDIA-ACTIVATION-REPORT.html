Section 5

Content Pillars — 5 Content Pillars

A structure that prevents asking "what should we post today?"

Every WCP post fits into one of five pillars. This isn’t a limitation — it’s a framework that ensures variety and consistency. Proportions: Educational 30%, Behind the Scenes 20%, Community 20%, Testimonials 15%, Promotional 15%.

📚
1. Educational — Psychoeducation
Therapy types, mental health facts, myths vs facts, psychological mechanisms. Builds expert positioning, gives value without expecting anything in return.
Examples: Carousel "5 mitów o depresji" · Reel "Co to jest EMDR?" · Post "CBT vs psychodynamiczna — jaka różnica?" · Story quiz "Jaki styl radzenia sobie ze stresem masz?"
🪞
2. Behind the Scenes
The clinic, the therapists, the process. We show that WCP is real people in a real place — not an abstract service. Builds trust and lowers the barrier to first contact.
Examples: Reel "Dzień z życia terapeuty WCP" · Carousel "Jak wygląda gabinet?" · Post "Poznaj [imię] — specjalizacja, filozofia pracy" · Therapist story takeover
👥
3. Community — Community & Events
Events, support groups, workshops, local initiatives. WCP isn’t just a service — it’s part of the community. This pillar builds belonging and loyalty.
Examples: Event "Otwarte Drzwi WCP" · Post about a new support group · Carousel "Jak działają grupy terapeutyczne?" · Live Q&A z terapeutą
⭐
4. Testimonials — Testimonials & Stories
Patient stories (anonymised), reviews, transformations. The most powerful conversion pillar — people trust people, not ads. Always fully anonymised with consent.
Examples: Post "Historia K.: Jak terapia zmieniła moje podejście do lęku" · Carousel "Co pacjenci mówią o WCP" · Story "Dlaczego zdecydowałem/am się na terapię" (anonimowo)
🎯
5. Promotional — Booking CTAs
Direct booking prompts, specialty information, availability, promotions. Not aggressive — but clear. Every post from this pillar has a specific CTA.
Examples: Post "Nowy terapeuta w zespole — [imię], specjalizacja: trauma" · Reel "Jak umówić wizytę online w 3 krokach" · Story countdown to group launch · Post "Wolne terminy — tydzień 15-21 kwietnia"

KPIs & Tracking — Metrics and Goals

What we measure, how often, and what we do with it
Rule: We do not measure everything. We measure what drives decisions. Likes are nice — but bookings are business. Every KPI must have an owner, a target, and a review cadence.

Monthly KPI Targets

Instagram Followers
+5–10%/mo
Target: 1,500 in 6 months (from ~300)
Engagement Rate
3–5%
IG health industry average: 2.5%. Target: above average.
Profile Visits → Website
15–20%
Conversion from profile to link click. Measures CTA effectiveness.
DM Inquiries
10–20/mo
Direct service inquiries. The most important leading indicator.
Story Completion
70%+
Percentage of viewers who watch stories to completion. A measure of content quality.
Reel Saves/Shares
50+/mo
Saves = value. Shares = virality. The most important Reels metrics.
Booking Attribution
Track
"How did you hear about us?" in the booking form. Social media as a source.
Facebook Event RSVPs
20+/event
A measure of local engagement and community building.

Review Cadence

FrequencyWhatWhoAction
WeeklyPost performance, DM responsesCommunity ManagerAdjust next week's content
MonthlyFull KPI dashboard reviewContent Strategist + KarolinaStrategy adjustments, new content ideas
QuarterlyDeep analysis: what worked, what didn't, ROIFull teamCalendar revision, budget review, pivot decisions

Quarterly Review Questions

  • Which posts had the highest engagement? Why?
  • Which day/time works best? (Adjust scheduling)
  • Which awareness dates resonated, and which "flopped"?
  • Are we attracting ideal patients or just likes?
  • What is our cost per inquiry (if we advertise)?
  • How many bookings come from social media vs other channels?

Booking Attribution — "How Did You Hear About Us?"

Add a "How did you hear about us?" field to the booking form (Booknetic). Options:

  • Instagram
  • Facebook
  • TikTok
  • Google Search
  • ZnanyLekarz
  • Friend referral
  • Other

This is the only reliable source of data on real social media ROI. Without it — we’re guessing.
