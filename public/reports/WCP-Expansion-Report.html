Infrastructure & Accessibility

  • Gminna Komunikacja Publiczna: Active bus service connecting all villages, resumed after Jan 2026 weather suspension

  • Distributed educational facilities across Gągławki, Pluski, Tomaszkowo

Play Therapy Room (25m²) — 4 Zones

🎨 Zone 1: Creative Expression (30%)

Art supplies, easel, craft table, display wall for children's work.

🎭 Zone 2: Imaginative Play (30%)

Sand tray station, puppet theater, dress-up corner, dollhouse/play kitchen.

🌿 Zone 3: Calm/Regulation (20%)

Book nook, soft seating, sensory tools, breathing/visualization aids.

⚡ Zone 4: Active Play (20%)

Movement space, building area, floor activities.

Play therapy equipment budget: PLN 12,000–18,000 for toys, sand tray miniatures, art supplies, puppets, sensory tools. One dedicated play therapy room serves 30–40 weekly slots (6–8 children/day, 5 days/week).

Safety Requirements (Mandatory)

  • Corner guards on all furniture and architectural corners
  • Anti-tip brackets for shelving and storage
  • Cordless window coverings — no blind cords
  • Tamper-resistant outlets with safety covers
  • Non-slip flooring throughout
  • HEPA filtration, sanitisation station at entrance
  • Secure locked storage for hazardous materials

Therapy Room Specifications

Individual Therapy (12–15m²)

2 ergonomic chairs + optional small couch. Dimmable lighting. Professional soundproofing (STC 50+): acoustic panels, solid-core doors with seals, dropped ceiling.

Soundproofing cost: 8,000–15,000 PLN per room (professional)

Couples Therapy (18–22m²)

Circular/triangular seating — no power positions. Movable furniture. Extra space for movement exercises. Neutral decor, balanced visual weight.

Room Requirements per Test

🧪 MOXO — Dedicated Space Required

Critical: Isolated, distraction-free room. No windows or covered windows. No visible clocks. Away from external noise.

  • Dedicated Windows computer, noise-canceling headphones, response device
  • Stable internet for cloud scoring, UPS backup
  • MOXO software license: 8,000–15,000 PLN/year

Total MOXO setup cost: 15,300–27,700 PLN

Schedule: Morning preferred (quietest), 20–25 min per test

📋 DIVA-5 — Can Share with Therapy Room

Quiet environment, 12–15m². Comfortable seating for clinician + patient (+ optional parent). 90–120 min sessions.

Computer/tablet for digital scoring, printed forms, timer.

📝 Conners-3 — Most Flexible

Portable, minimal space requirements. Parent/teacher/self-report questionnaires + clinician interpretation. Can be done in any quiet space — even a waiting area if private.

Risk Considerations

⚠️ Transportation Dependency

Patients primarily car-dependent. Parking is essential. Winter weather may affect accessibility (Jan 2026 transport suspension precedent). Ensure adequate parking and clear winter access plan.

⚠️ Market Education

Play therapy and ADHD diagnostics are not widely understood by general public. Budget for educational marketing content and school outreach programmes.

⚠️ Diagnostic Specialist Scarcity

DIVA-5 / MOXO / Conners-3 certified specialists are scarce in the region. Begin recruitment early — this could be the actual bottleneck, not space or patients.
