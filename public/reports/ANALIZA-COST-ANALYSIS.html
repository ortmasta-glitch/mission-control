Critical Methodology Warning

Understanding what ANALIZA data actually represents
⚠️ CRITICAL: ANALIZA Column E ("Przychody") is NOT therapy revenue — it is 80-95% tax payments (ZUS, PIT-4R, PIT-36L). Using ANALIZA "Przychody" as revenue would lead to catastrophic strategic errors. True revenue comes from bank statements only.

💡 Structural Shift Analysis

The dramatic drop from 428K (2023) to 179K (2024) reflects a strategic shift from UoP (employment contracts) to B2B (contractor) arrangements.

Financial Impact: ~200K+ PLN annual savings in direct employer costs

Why the savings: Elimination of employer-side ZUS (approx. 20% of gross), paid leave obligations, severance requirements, and sick pay.

Important: B2B contractors set their own rates — this doesn't mean therapists earn less. The savings come from structural efficiency, not salary reduction.

Section 06

Key Findings & Recommendations

Strategic insights from 40 months of cost data
1

UoP → B2B Shift Saved ~200K+ PLN Annually

The structural transition from employment contracts to B2B arrangements eliminated employer-side ZUS obligations and reduced compliance overhead. This is a permanent efficiency gain that compounds yearly.

2

Operating Cost Ratio Improved from 41% to 23%

Despite revenue growth, WCP has become significantly more operationally efficient. Cost control is excellent — maintain current discipline while scaling.

3

2026 ZUS Spike Requires Attention

ZUS payments tracking 49% higher in Q1 2026 vs 2025. Investigate: Is this due to new UoP hires? Regulatory changes? Higher salary base? Budget impact could be 200K+ PLN if sustained.

4

EU Project Income is a Strategic Opportunity

115K PLN from government contracts in 2025 shows WCP can access public funding. Pursue systematically — this diversifies revenue and builds institutional relationships.

5

Software Costs Rising — Monitor ROI

Software/subscription costs grew from 42K (2023) to 56K (2025). As WCP scales tools (Booknetic, analytics, marketing platforms), ensure each has measurable ROI.

Recommendations

🎯 Immediate Actions
  • Review 2026 ZUS spike with accountant — understand drivers and budget impact
  • Audit software subscriptions — cancel unused, optimize used
  • Document B2B contractor management process for compliance
📈 Strategic Opportunities
  • Systematically pursue EU/local government contracts — 100K+ revenue potential
  • Negotiate volume discounts with recurring vendors (marketing, software)
  • Consider shared service center for admin to further optimize costs at scale

Limitations
