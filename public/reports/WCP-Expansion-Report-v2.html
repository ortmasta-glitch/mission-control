Karolina's Guidelines — Vision and Requirements

Direct knowledge from WCP founder
Philosophy: Minimal costs, maximum effect. The location should be move-in ready — painting, furniture, toys. No structural changes, no property purchase.

Key Requirements

1

One location: Bartąg

We're not considering multiple locations — focusing on the Bartąg/Stawiguda area, close to Olsztyn. This is a developing suburb with young families.

2

Rental, not purchase

Apartment rental — flexibility, lower initial costs, ability to change location if the business needs more space.

3

Apartment layout = clinic

Two rooms (bedrooms) = play therapy rooms. Separate kitchen = staff room. Hallway = waiting area. Minimum 50–60 m², 3+ rooms.

4

Move-in ready

Minimal fit-out costs: only painting, furniture, toys/equipment. No structural changes, no walls, no installations.

5

CCPT Equipment — detailed list

Full list of toys and equipment for play therapy (Child-Centered Play Therapy) needed. Preference: wood over plastic. Some toys can be used (OLX, Allegro, Vinted).

Why This Model Makes Sense

  • Low financial risk — deposit + first month rent + furniture + toys = 25–35k PLN, not 180–330k like with purchase/renovation
  • Quick start — apartment move-in ready, painting 2–3 days, IKEA furniture in a week, toys online
  • Flexibility — if the location doesn't work, we change. If we need more space, we move
  • Market test — confirm demand for play therapy in Bartąg before larger investment

Why Bartąg is Ideal for CCPT

🏠 Young families

Bartąg and surrounding areas are one of the fastest growing regions near Olsztyn. New housing developments = young families with preschool and early school-age children (4–12 years) — the main CCPT target group.

🚗 Accessibility

Direct bus connection to Olsztyn (MZK). Main artery ul. Olsztyńska/Bartąska. Parents drive children after work — 15–20 min from Olsztyn center. Parking by the building is a must-have.

🏫 Schools = Referral Source

Zespół Szkolno-Przedszkolny in Stawiguda, schools in Bartąg — without dedicated psychological services with diagnosis. WCP can become the main school partner in the area.

💰 Lower costs

Bartąg apartment rent: 1,500–2,500 PLN/month. Compare: Olsztyn center 3,500–7,000 PLN/month. Difference = 2,000–4,500 PLN/month savings from day one.

What to Check During Viewings

  • Room layout — are bedrooms separated by doors? Kitchen separate?
  • Paint condition — is refresh enough or full painting needed?
  • Parking — is there space under the building? Do neighbors block it?
  • Quiet — are neighbors loud? Street noise? Therapy sessions require peace
  • Owner — do they agree to business activity at the address? Critical!
  • Accessibility — ground floor or elevator
  • Windows — blinds/curtains for light control (important for MOXO)

Rent Negotiation Tips

1

Propose longer contract

2–3 years with guaranteed rent increase cap = lower base rent. Owners prefer stability.

2

Explain the business

Children's therapy = quiet, peaceful tenants. No parties, low usage. Owners like this.

3

Agree who pays utilities

For 50–60 m²: electricity ~200–350 PLN, water ~80–150 PLN, heating ~150–300 PLN winter. Rent with utilities = easier budgeting.

4

Deposit = 1 month, not 3

Negotiate deposit to 1 month rent. Standard is 1–2 months. 3 months = excessive.

Therapy Room Color Scheme

  • Walls: Light, warm tones — white, cream, pastel green or blue. Avoid bright colors (overstimulate children, hinder focus)
  • Floor: Keep existing — rug/play mat defines play area
  • Lighting: Warm, adjustable. Lamps on shelves. Avoid fluorescent lights

Break-even

Monthly break-even: ~14 sessions/month (average 250 PLN/session = 3,500 PLN revenue) covers fixed costs (rent + utilities). With therapist (55%): ~30 sessions/month = 7,500 PLN covers fixed costs + therapist. That's ~7–8 sessions/week — achievable in month 2–3.
