🔴 Critical Gap #1: No Psychiatrist

WCP cannot prescribe medication or offer psychiatric assessment. This is WCP's #1 capability gap vs ANIMA and limits:
  • Independent ADHD diagnosis pathway (must refer out)
  • Medication management for patients
  • Crisis intervention requiring pharmacological support
  • Insurance partnerships requiring psychiatric coverage

Recommendations

Priority hiring and training actions

Priority Actions

P1

Recruit a Psychiatrist

CRITICAL This is the single highest-leverage strategic move. A psychiatrist on staff enables: independent ADHD diagnosis pathway, medication management, insurance partnerships, and parity with ANIMA's offering.

Target: August 2026 start
Budget: 15-25K PLN/month (B2B or part-time initially)
Job posting: Ready for distribution

P2

First Wave: 6 Therapists into CBT/Gestalt Schools

HIGH IMPACT Expands certified capacity in the two most demanded modalities. Prioritize recent graduates with strong clinical potential.

Candidates: Julia Dąbrowska, Kamila Świniarska, Katarzyna Grzegorczyk, Marta Kmieć, Marta Minko-Jankowska, Beata Korycka
Cost: 180-240K PLN over 4 years
ROI: 720K–1.07M PLN over 3 years

P2

Add 2-3 Couples Therapists

REVENUE OPPORTUNITY Couples therapy at 500 PLN/session represents a 2.5× revenue multiplier vs individual sessions. Current capacity (4 therapists) is insufficient for demand.

Approach: Train existing therapists OR recruit specialists
Revenue potential: 384K–576K PLN/year

P3

Group Therapy Facilitator Training

MEDIUM PRIORITY Group therapy is high-margin (150 PLN/session per participant) and underutilized at WCP. 5 therapists nominated.

Supervision budget: 6,600–7,900 PLN/month ongoing

P3

Systemic Family Therapist

MEDIUM PRIORITY Currently only Anna Piotrowska covers family therapy. Single point of failure risk.

Long-Term Development Framework

📈 Investment → Revenue Framework

HR development is not a cost center — it's a revenue multiplier. Every therapist who gains certification unlocks:

  • Higher session rates (20-30% increase)
  • New patient segments (couples, families, groups)
  • Insurance panel eligibility
  • Reduced supervision dependency
  • Competitive differentiation

Year 1 total investment: 426K–580K PLN
3-year projected return: 720K–1.07M PLN
