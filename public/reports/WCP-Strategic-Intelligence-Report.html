Profile Unification Plan

All WCP social media profiles unified under a consistent visual identity:

  • Color scheme: Dark theme, gold accents (matching this report)
  • Bios: Consistent across platforms — same tagline, same contact info, same link
  • Imagery: No stock photos. Real therapists, real spaces, warm lighting
  • Logo: Crown + WCP wordmark prominently placed

Platform Strategy

📸 Instagram — Carousels + Reels

Primary audience: 25-40, aesthetic-aware, already consuming mental health content.

  • Carousels: Education pillars — "5 signs you should talk to a specialist", "CBT vs Gestalt — which therapy is right for you?"
  • Reels: Short-form therapist tips, myth-busting, "day in the life" content
  • Stories: Daily presence — polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes, booking reminders
👥 Facebook — Community + Events

Primary audience: 30-55, parents, professionals, life-transition demographics.

  • Community posts: Longer-form educational content, therapist spotlights
  • Facebook Live: Monthly Q&A sessions with therapists
  • Events: Webinars, open days, workshops
  • Facebook Group: "Psychological Support — Warmia and Mazury" (moderated)
🎵 TikTok — Therapy Tips & Myth-Busting

Primary audience: 18-30, Gen Z, high mental health content engagement.

  • Format: Authentic, phone-shot, therapist-on-camera content
  • Topics: "Myths about therapy", "POV: Your first session", "3 techniques for a panic attack"
  • Tone: Real, warm, zero corporate polish. Authenticity > production value
  • Pilot: 4 videos from 2 willing therapists → measure before scaling
