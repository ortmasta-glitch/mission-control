Section 02

P1: Psychiatrist Recruitment

Why this is priority #1 · Compensation · Onboarding plan
⚠️ Why This is Priority #1: WCP cannot prescribe medication or offer psychiatric evaluation. Main competitor ANIMA has an on-staff psychiatrist — this is the biggest service gap. Patients requiring medication (ADHD, severe depression, anxiety disorders, bipolar disorder) must be referred out.

Compensation Benchmarking — Polish Psychiatry Market 2026

SettingMonthly Gross (PLN)Annual Gross (PLN)Notes
NFZ full-time12,000-18,000144,000-216,000Base + overtime, low retention
Private clinic (salary)20,000-30,000240,000-360,000Typical private range
Private clinic (revenue share)60-70% per sessionVariablePopular model — 250-350 PLN/session
ANIMA (estimate)25,000-35,000300,000-420,000Competitive private offer

Recommended Compensation Model

  • Base salary: 15,000-18,000 PLN gross/month (guaranteed minimum)
  • Revenue share: 60% per session (250-300 PLN/session → 150-180 PLN for psychiatrist)
  • Monthly target: 25,000-35,000 PLN gross at full schedule
  • Benefits: flexible hours, administrative support, ready patient flow, modern space

Onboarding Plan (First 90 Days)

1

Welcome (Week 1)

Orientation with Ewelina, meet the therapy team, clinic tour, system access (Booknetic, records), workstation setup.

2

Integration (Weeks 2-4)

Shadowing with 2-3 therapists (CBT + psychodynamic), attend team meeting, begin schedule, establish patient handoff protocols.

3

Ramp-up (Weeks 5-8)

Full patient schedule (target 60%), begin psychiatric assessments, coordinate pharmacotherapy with therapists.

4

Full Productivity (Weeks 9-12)

Full schedule (80-100%), established patient referral flow, participate in team case conferences.
